"NATO ready to assist Czechoslovakia in fight against Black Cholera"
- NATO Secretary General

For the past 16 years, the Black Cholera bacterium has been raging in Czechoslovakia. For such a long time, NATO has only managed to contain the spread, protecting the rest of Europe from the horror that has engulfed the Czech region..
